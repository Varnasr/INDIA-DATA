Key Research Findings

Critical Pattern Identified: Strong negative correlation (r = -0.74, p < 0.001) between voter deletions per booth and INDIA Alliance performance. Across 77,292 analyzed booths: INDIA 38.7%, NDA 49.9%, Others 11.4%.

🔍 Statistical Evidence: Systematic gradient where increasing deletions-per-booth correlates with decreased INDIA win share. Low-deletion booths (1-99): INDIA 39.6% vs High-deletion booths (≥250): INDIA 31.8% — a significant 7.8 percentage point drop.
