Research Findings Overview

Systematic Electoral Pattern Analysis: Across 77,292 booths with alliance coding, we observe stark win share patterns: INDIA 38.7%, NDA 49.9%, Others 11.4%. Critical finding: Strong negative correlation (r = -0.74) between voter deletions per booth and INDIA Alliance performance, indicating potential systematic manipulation patterns affecting democratic outcomes.
